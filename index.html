About me

Building systems that scale.

I'm a software engineer with 10+ years of experience building high-performance, reliable systems at the intersection of databases, distributed systems, and cloud infrastructure.

Engineering philosophy: I care deeply about correctness, performance, and the kind of engineering that makes systems invisible to the people who depend on them.
